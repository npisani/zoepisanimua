About Zoe

Zoe Pisani is a professional make-up artist, based in North Yorkshire. Zoe specialises in weddings, commercial, and TV/Film.

Growing up, Zoe loved watching horror films and developed a curiosity about how the effects for the movies were made. That curiosity later developed into a professional interest and passion for make-up so she went on to train professionally at Craven College where she studied Theatrical and Media Make-up, coming out with a distinction for her work.

Whilst Zoe was training, she did work for a jewellery catalogue, BBC Talent and then headed the make-up department for her first horror film. Since college Zoe has travelled throughout the UK and Europe to work at weddings, commercial, TV and film. She is highly passionate about her work and the happiness of her clients.
