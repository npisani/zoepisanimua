Welcome

Based in North Yorkshire, Zoe Pisani is a professional make-up artist with over 6 years of industry experience. With training in airbrush, special effects and wigs, Zoe has her own kit and is willing to travel in order to meet your wedding, event, editorial/commercial, TV and film needs.

Zoe takes fantastic care and shows great attention to detail when applying the makeup on every individual client and likes every bride to have a fun experience they can take away with them and treasure their big day.
