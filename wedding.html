Wedding

Your wedding day is one of the most important days of your life. Every bride wants to look and feel her best. The photos from your wedding day will be pictures you keep forever.

Zoe offers a 1.5 hour trial day where you can come to her or stay in the comfort of your own home. She will spend 1.5 hours getting to know more about you, what style of makeup you are wanting on your day and working with you to get the perfect look you are happy with.

On your big day Zoe will travel to your desired location where she will make you feel calm and relaxed, whilst making you look your best and ready for the big day ahead.

All services include airbrush makeup and false lashes, if desired. Services are also available to any member of your bridal party. Please see prices page for a list of all bridal packages, if you have any further questions please contact Zoe via the contact form link below.
